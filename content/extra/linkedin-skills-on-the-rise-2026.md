# Skills on the Rise: The Fastest-Growing Skills in 2026

LinkedIn reports that the fastest-growing skills are identified across 12 markets using year-over-year growth in two signals:

- Skill acquisition (skills added to profiles)
- Hiring success (skills held by members who were hired)

The main message is that employers increasingly prioritize demonstrated skills over degrees, job titles, or linear career paths.

## Why This Matters

Work and hiring expectations are shifting from credentials to capabilities. LinkedIn also notes that one in five professionals globally feel that lacking the right skills makes job search harder.

## 2026 Global Skill Trends

### People skills are rising

Leadership and communication capabilities are increasingly important:

- Cross-Functional Collaboration
- Team Management
- Mentorship
- Public Speaking
- Cross-Functional Coordination

### Business growth skills are rising

In competitive markets, organizations value commercial execution:

- Go-to-Market Strategy
- Business Development

### AI skills are expanding beyond coding

Demand is growing for both technical AI and strategic business application of AI:

- Prompt Engineering
- Large Language Models
- AI business strategy and AI integration

### Risk and compliance skills are rising

As environments become more complex, demand increases for:

- Governance
- Risk Management
- Compliance

## Practical Career Actions

- Prioritize one people skill, one AI skill, and one business skill in your learning plan.
- Show proof of skill with projects, outcomes, and portfolio-style evidence.
- Update your profile based on measurable capability, not only role titles.
- Align skill development with roles and markets where demand is accelerating.
